Virginia has the Atlantic Ocean as it's eastern border and the Appalachian Mountains to the west.
The land that Virginia sits on has history that dates thousands of years prior to colonization.
Virginia was colonized by Europeans around 1607 and the Jamestown settlement was established.
Virginia was one of the original thirteen colonies.
This site takes a brief look at three of Virginia's major cities, Richmond, Virginia Beach, and Lynchburg.
