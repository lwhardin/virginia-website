Virginia has the Atlantic Ocean as it's east border and the Appalachian Mountains to the west.
The land that Virginia sits on has history that dates thousands of years prior to colonization.
Virginia was colonized by Europeans around 1607 and the Jamestown settlement was established. Virginia was one of the original thirteen colonies.

Richmond

Richmond is the capital of Virginia and was incorporated in 1742. The beautiful city sits on the James River.

Virginia Beach

Virginia Beach sits on the Atlantic Ocean and is the most populous city in Virginia. VIrginia Beach became a city in 1952.

Lynchburg

Lynchburg was settled in 1757. Lynchburg has a population of around 80,000 people.
